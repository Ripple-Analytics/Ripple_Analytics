**Why Critical**: Most people misapply ensemble statistics to individual decisions, leading to ruin. This model corrects a fundamental error in probability thinking.

### Reflexivity (George Soros)
**Why Critical**: Markets and social systems have feedback loops between perception and reality. This explains bubbles, crashes, and boom-bust cycles.

**Application**: Identify self-reinforcing trends; recognize unsustainable dynamics; time entries/exits.

### Convexity (Nassim Taleb)
**Why Critical**: Non-linear payoffs are everywhere but often misunderstood. Convex strategies benefit from volatility.

**Application**: Seek convex exposures; avoid concave risks; embrace volatility when convex.
